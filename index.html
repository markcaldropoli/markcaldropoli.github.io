About Me

I am a Freshman Computer Science Major at Binghamton University. I am interested in software development and information security. I currently know Java, am teaching myself Python in my free time, and learning C and Assembly Language in class. I am a Campus Ambassador for #HackHarassment, an organization dedicated to solving the issue of online bullying. At Binghamton, I am a member of HackBU, Binghamton ACM, Spoon Bing, and The BUZoo. I love hackathons and enjoy developing new creations to help society.

Hackers vs. Harassment

HackAE Collaborative Hackathon Project - Mark Caldropoli & Emily Lakic

Hackers vs. Harassment was created in an effort to improve the overpowering societal issue of bullying by collecting data using Python and presenting it in the form of a website to illustrate the complexity of the problem. Data was collected to make pie charts on the website based on questions asked in our survey. Received the #HackHarassment award from Major League Hacking at HackAE held at Binghamton University's Innovative Technologies Complex.
